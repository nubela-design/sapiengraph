B2B enrichment in Spreadsheet with LLM personalizations

Try these formulas in action

  • A2

    Cell

    Enrich person's profile data

    =SG_PERSON(profile_url, attribute)

  • A3

    Cell

    Enrich person's by role

    =SG_LOOKUP_ROLE("ceo", "microsoft")

  • A4

    Cell

    Enrich person's by work history

    =SG_PERSON_WORK(profile_url, attribute)

  • B1

    Cell

    Enrich company's profile data

    =SG_COMPANY(company_url, attribute)

  • B2

    Cell

    Enrich company's by total employee count

    =SG_EMPLOYEE_COUNT(company_url)

  • B3

    Cell

    Enrich company's by name

    =SG_LOOKUP_COMPANY("Apple")

  • C1

    Cell

    Enrich contact by email addresses

    =SG_PERSONAL_EMAILS(profile_url)

  • C2

    Cell

    Enrich contact by phone numbers

    =SG_PERSONAL_NUMBERS(profile_url)

  • C3

    Cell

    Enrich contact by profile from email

    =SG_REVERSE_EMAIL_LOOKUP(email)

    Cell

  • D2

    Cell

    =SG_COMPANY_SEARCH(country, max_results)

    Cell
